Featured Music

Featured songs:

"D>E>A>T>H>M>E>T>A>L" by Panchiko

If you are searching for a song that lies at the intersection of dream pop and indie rock, you should check out the song "D>E>A>T>H>M>E>T>A>L" by the artist Panchiko. This song possesses a dreamy, ethereal quality that allows a listener to reimagine their environment around them to the scene of a movie.

"Walking the Cow" by Daniel Johnston

If you are searching for a song that provides a calming aura, check out the song "Walking the Cow" by Daniel Johnston. This song provides an almost whiny vintage sound that makes you feel like a Napolean Dynamite character.

"Cut Me" by Moses Sumney

This song is brilliant in its vocal range and instrumentation. Moses possesses a unique sound that is soulful and deep. This song feels warm and goosebumps your skin with the distincive audio arena it creates.

"Falling out of the Sky" by Armand Hammer

This song comes from the album Haram, which caused a large shock factor in the music community due to the album cover being quite explicit. The album cover featues two bloody pig heads, which is relevant to the title of this hip hop album being called "Haram." The song itself has a unique lyrical flow that forces listeners to bop their heads. Backing up the skillful rap flow, the song is supported by ineteresting audio samples that enhance the experience of listening to the song.

"Center of Gravity" by Yo La Tengo

This song features a long of distinct instrumentation that transports you to summer nights watching a live band perform at a local sea-side restaurant. The soft voices lullaby listeners to sway to the loving and warm beat.

"Life Worth Missing" by Car Seat Headrest

This song is an indie rock feature that channels a listener to feel like the main character in a coming of age film. This song in my opinion is a song to air scream to while you put your headphones on full volume.

"Platinum Tears" by Baby Father

If you want to either impress or shock your friends, play this beat in front of them. "Platinum Tears" is a british rap song that has impressive lyrical flow. The most defining feature of this song is that it sounds like an artist rapping in a british accent.

"Blue Motel Room" by Joni Mitchell

This somber song cradles listeners while Joni softly whimpers about blues. The song provides a comforting mood that cushions listers while they dissociate in a blues mood.

"Sober to Death" by Car Seat Headrest

This song is the epitome of college indie rock. The song allows you to purge your emotions while singing along to the adolescent punk sound of Car Seat Headrest.
